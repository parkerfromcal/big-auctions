About

BIC Auctions, Inc. is a family owned business with over 35 years of experience in the auction industry. We are a full service auction company specializing in concrete construction. We help owners at all stages of their business, including those looking to retire, sell their business or market excess equipment.

Our extensive experience in the field and vast knowledge of the industry, allow us to get the highest recovery value for our customers’ assets. From thorough preparation of assets to detailed cataloging, you’ll see how our expertise makes a difference long before the day of the sale.
